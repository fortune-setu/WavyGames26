Wavy Games is a sports fundraiser that brings people together for a fun and friendly competition while raising money for important charities around the world.

This event is also a tribute to my dear classmate, Avu “Wavy” Idris. Avu loved sports and had a big heart. He was the kind of person who would help anyone if he had the chance. Wavy Games is our way of honouring his energy and kindness by doing something active, positive and meaningful.

📍 Event Details

Date: 19th March 2026
Time: 4.30PM-6.30PM
Location: Regional Sports Centre (RSC), Cork Road, Waterford

💙 Why We’re Doing This

Every euro raised will be shared equally between three amazing charities:

🌍 Compassion International
Compassion supports children living in poverty by providing access to education, healthcare, food and essential life skills. Your support helps give children the opportunity for a brighter future.

🩸 International Sickle Cell Fund
The International Sickle Cell Fund raises awareness and provides support for people living with sickle cell disease. They work to improve healthcare access, fund treatment and support families affected by this condition.

🤍 LaunchGood – Palestine Campaign
LaunchGood helps raise emergency funds for families in Palestine who need urgent support, including food, medical aid and basic necessities during times of crisis.

All money raised will be split equally and donated directly to these three causes.
